Parkell Product Launch

This project involved launching Predicta Bioactive—a revolutionary, new product—into the dental industry. It was crucial to develop a design system that could be flexible in different mediums while maintaining solid product recognition. Aegis, a dental publication company that performs studies of ad recognition, awarded a series of Predicta magazine ads a distinction as one of the most recognizable product ads in their magazines. This launch depended on print and digital media, including but not limited to magazine ads, web banners, email marketing, social media marketing and video to educate the intended audience about a new product advancement.
